What is No Food Waste Web App?

We are a non-profit enabling the exchange of excess food from the food industry to those who need it most. Farms, Food Vendors, Supermarket, and Individuals post excess food on No Food Waste Web App while we pick up and deliver it to varoius beneficiaries; Charity Organizations.
