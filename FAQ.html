What service does no food waste provide ?

No Food Waste is a social enterprise that connects food businesses that have large volumes of surplus food, such as farms, manufacturers and distributors, to charities that need it.

Where is No Food Waste App being used?

No Food Waste app is being used in Nigeria, predominantly in lagos.
Our aim is to offer our solution to communities around the world who can benefit and achieve our vision of a world where no good food goes to waste and we are currently interested in hearing from charities and businesses globally that could benefit from our solution.

Does No Food Waste accept hot or prepared food for charity ?

We are not currently able to accept hot food for distribution to charities, or prepared food from restaurants and hotels.

What type of food does No Food Waste accept from suppliers and give to charity ?

If a charity is collecting food directly from a local retailer through No Food Waste app the food received will mainly be surplus food. This includes bakery products, fresh fruit, vegetables and some chilled foods. The amount of food varies from supplier to supplier, as does the food type.
If a charity is collecting food from us directly, the type of food is generally; fresh short dated products, products with slightly damaged packaging or products that were ordered in excess due to error by a manufacturer or retailer, good but unmarketable farm produce. The food we collect from the food suppliers can include raw food stuff, fresh food, refrigerated and frozen food, dried food, food in boxes, cans and bottles. The food varies week to week.

Who can supply food to No Food Waste?

We accept food from farmers/growers, food vendors, food product manufacturers, supermarket, and anyone with surpplus or good but unwanted food stuff.

What are benefits for charity ?

No Food Waste partered with charities which provide support to many individuals within the community, including children and youth at risk of food poverty, the elderly, those who are homeless, those who are going through drug and alcohol rehabilitation, women who are escaping domestic violence and families with low incomes who need help to get by.

Is my charity eligible ?

No Food Waste works with charities and not-for-profits who have a food budget, and who are in a position to receive, store, prepare and serve food safely. After the organisation has gone through the application process successfully, they and our team will discuss whether delivery/collection is needed. Based on this and the volume of food available, they will then either be placed on a waiting list or set up on the system as a charity partner. pls sign-up here to get started.

What is the benefit of No Food Waste solution ?

The modern food system is unpredictable. Many distributors and producers of food find themselves with large volumes of surplus due to order cancellations, mistakes in packaging and a variety of other conditions, making perfectly good food unsuitable for retailers. This high quality food is often wasted at a large cost to businesses. Good food that can feed people should never be wasted. we offer businesses a solution by using this food to support the work of charity sector.
    The benefits to the food industry include:
  • Reduction in waste disposal costs
  • Reduction of carbon footprint
  • Fulfillment of Origin Green targets
  • Positive Brand sentiment
  • Strengthening community relationships
  • Increased staff engagement and fulfillment
